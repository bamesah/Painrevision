About PainRevision

Built by a trainee, for every trainee that follows.

PainRevision was started by a pain medicine trainee who passed the exams the hard way — navigating a fragmented landscape of resources, outdated textbooks, and a near-total absence of video tutorials that trainees in other specialities take for granted.

Pain medicine is a complex, multidisciplinary speciality. Yet when it comes to exam preparation, trainees are largely left to piece things together alone — no central hub, no consistent resource library, no community. PainRevision was built to change that.
