01 — The Mark

A symbol for revision itself

The logo had to work tiny — as an app icon, a YouTube avatar, a favicon. We explored four routes around the idea of learning & revision. The Revision Loop is the recommended primary; the others remain in your back pocket.

Primary

The Revision Loop

An open ring — to review again and again — with the spark of understanding clicking into place.

Rising Progress

Three climbing bars — measurable improvement, scores going up.

The Flashcard

A turning card with a spark — direct nod to the MCQ revision app.

The P-Monogram

The letter P with the idea held inside — compact and ownable.

02 — Why it works

The meaning behind the loop

An open ring + a spark

Revision is repetition — you loop back over the material until it sticks. The ring captures that cycle; the warm dot is the moment it finally clicks. Together they read as effort rewarded.

  • 1

    The open loop

    Built on a single circle, opened at the top. Geometric and instantly recognisable at 16px.

  • 2

    The spark

    Amber, resting in the gap — insight, the answer, the "got it" feeling.

  • 3

    Round, friendly caps

    Soft terminals keep it warm and approachable, never clinical or cold.
